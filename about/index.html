Alexei Vanyashin

Typeface designer based in Almaty, Kazakhstan. Graduated Type&Typography at BHSAD Moscow in 2010. He has worked closely with Swiss Typefaces, Optimo, Dalton Maag, and Google Fonts for over 10 years, spoken on typographic events from Madrid to Hong Kong. Currently he is Cyrillic Scipt Chair at Granshan Foundation, runs Cyreal Type Foundry.

His interest in letterforms sparkled since 2001 during his academic studies in Graphic Design, and coincided with his curiosity in learning languages. Drawing letters with linguistic awareness brings more sense to the process.

Personal type projects: Florian Geo Text Junge Schmale Antique Adamina Vidaloka

As a native Cyrillic specialist he consulted on many Cyrillic type projects including Lush Handwritten Prometo Soleto Roboto Noto Sans Shantell Sans Montserrat Cormorant Exo 2 Bitter Pacifico EB Garamond Ysabeau Cera(2015) Volkorn Alegreya and Alegreya Sans Kurale Next Pan Practice Pan Programme Pan Px Grotesk Pan Theinhardt Bahnschrift, and many others...

At Google Fonts his Cyrillic work includes: Nunito and Nunito Sans with Manvel Shmavonyan, Rubik and Rubik Mono One Lora with Olga Karpushina, Mulish Raleway Libre Franklin Lobster with Gayaneh Bagdasaryan, Caveat and Amatic SC with Eugene Spizhovy, Play Yanone Kaffeesatz

His input on Cyrillic was beneficial for foundries like Optimo, Dalton Maag, TypeMates, Mota Italic, ArrowType, Huerta Tipográfica, Catharsis Fonts, &DISCOVER, Google Fonts

He collaborated on Cyrillic Extentions with Swiss Typefaces: Euclid TF Stockholm Type SangBleu Sans Suisse Int'l Condensed Simplon PZSP New Paris Skyline, Newlyn: New Rubrik, facetype Strangelove, PhilaType: Merge, TypeBy (formely OurType): Coutea, Schriftlabor: Tirol Sans
