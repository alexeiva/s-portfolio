<h1 data-testid="page-title">Alexei Vanyashin</h1>

Typeface designer based in Almaty, Kazakhstan. Graduated Type&Typography at BHSAD Moscow in 2010.
He has worked closely with Swiss Typefaces, Optimo, Dalton Maag, and Google Fonts for over 10 years, spoken on typographic events from Madrid to Hong Kong. Currently he is Cyrillic Scipt Chair at Granshan Foundation, runs <a href="http://cyreal.org">Cyreal Type Foundry</a>.

His interest in letterforms sparkled since 2001 during his academic studies in Graphic Design, and coincided with his curiosity in learning languages. Drawing letters with linguistic awareness brings more sense to the process. 

Personal type projects: <a class="fontlist" href="/florian">Florian</a> <a class="fontlist" href="#">Geo&nbsp;Text</a> <a class="fontlist" href="http://cyreal.org/fonts/junge/">Junge</a> <a class="fontlist" href="/schmale-antique">Schmale&nbsp;Antique</a> <a class="fontlist" href="http://cyreal.org/fonts/adamina/">Adamina</a> <a class="fontlist" href="http://cyreal.org/fonts/vidaloka/">Vidaloka</a>
